 I am now living in an environment where I have the opportunity to improve myself tremendously, and I hope that by the end of this week I can move on with my life as a new person.

sometimes people can't develop themselves the way they should because they are too comfortable with the people in the environment they are in, so spending a few weeks a year with different people to completely develop themselves makes the person maybe 10 times, maybe 100 times better, more productive, more hardworking.

That's why spending time with people with whom we have differences other than our main goal will make you more grounded and more fundamentally a good person.
