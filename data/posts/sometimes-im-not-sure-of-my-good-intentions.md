title: "Sometimes I'm not sure of my good intentions"
date: "2023-03-12"
author: "ISA"
---

 _Sometimes I'm not sure of my good intentions. I know I'm not a bad person, but sometimes I just don't have that much empathy and forgiveness in my thoughts. Sometimes I can make very emotional decisions, and sometimes I can make decisions completely out of my emotions. I don't know if this is a bad thing or if I can control it. I hope in the future I can control my emotional instability. If I don't balance my emotions and reason correctly, I can turn into a bad person I don't want in the future. It saddens me to hear that I cannot find this balance from the people I love and respect. But I'm so sure of my thoughts that it's like I wouldn't admit it if everyone told me it was wrong._

_İ.S.A_
