 _I would like to explain with a very short example that writing is much more difficult than we think. I'm going to prove how talented the writers are and that they have talents that normal people can never have. The authors I mentioned are not those who lost their importance after a few years, but those who are still remembered and read hundreds of years later._ 

_The example I want to give is this:_ 

_If I hand you a pen and paper and ask you to write down what you see or what you think. How many pages can you write? Or if I asked you to tell me a story, how many minutes or how many hours can you tell me the same story? Maybe I'm not a writer who can describe what's on my mind in a few sentences. Actually, I'm not a writer. I just don't want my thoughts to be forgotten even by myself. Anyway, our topic was how difficult or even impossible writing is for most people. When reading a long novel, it fascinates me that one can create so many emotions and thoughts for us by telling them. The novels I am talking about are not short, they are hundreds of pages, and some novel series total more than a thousand pages. I think writing here is the art of putting into words what the human mind cannot comprehend. Although it is difficult for me to reach that level, I think it is not impossible. We can talk about the existence of something as impossible for most people, but I'm not like most people._
