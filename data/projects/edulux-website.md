The official website for Edulux, an educational association in Luxembourg.

## Overview
Edulux is an association focused on cultural integration and education for people of all age groups. They needed a digital presence to share their mission, announce events, and connect with the community in Luxembourg. You can view the live site at [eduluxasbl.lu](http://eduluxasbl.lu).

## Features
- **Accessible Design**: Built to be easily navigable by users of all ages and technical backgrounds.
- **Information Architecture**: Clearly outlines the services, cultural programs, and educational offerings provided by the association.
- **Responsive Layout**: Works seamlessly across mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

## Outcome
Providing a digital storefront for an NGO required focusing on clear communication and performance. The resulting website successfully serves as the central hub for the association's online activities.
