ComposeX is a manual writing copilot built for X/Twitter workflows.

The idea was simple: instead of writing into an empty box, I wanted a cleaner workflow that starts from real posts. ComposeX monitors a curated live feed, lets you pick a visible source post, and then generates both a reply and an original post inspired by that context.

It is intentionally manual. It does not auto-publish, does not connect directly to the X publish API, and does not require Twitter authentication. The goal is to support the writing process without removing the human from it.

### Features
- Curated live feed of real visible X posts
- Reply generation from selected source posts
- Original post generation inspired by live context
- Gemini-powered content generation
- Manual-first workflow with no auto-posting
- Clean MVP focused on writing speed and clarity
